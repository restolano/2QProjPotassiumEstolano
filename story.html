History

In 2010, Becarios de Santo Tomas, the Sole Scholars Association of
the University of Santo Tomas, has a project entitled "Iskolars Ng
Mga Scholars" which was in its 5th year by that time. By reason of
the upcoming Quadricentennial year of the university, the organization
has decided to elevate their project and share it to different Thomasian
organizations until they have come up with "Iskolars Ng Mga Tomasino: 400 Scholars @ 400!"
proposal. The Office for Community Development(currently UST Simbahayan Community
Development Office) suggested for the revision of project title since the
feasibility of having 400 scholars is difficult to achieve. The project became "BUTIL"
which stands for Bonded University Towards Imbued Learning. BUTIL was inspired
from the 'Parable of the Seeds' in the Bible. On August 2010, BUTIL was
presented before the UST Office for Student Affairs(OSA) and they
adopted it as one of the four projects of USTbongrunong, a students' initiative
led by Central Student Council. Both became the primary partners
of Becarios de Santo Tomas for the BUTIL Scholarship Program Program.
Several months had been spent for preparations and meetings
until majority of commitments was finalized on January 2011.
The number of beneficiaries was determined after the number of commitments
has been finalized. Last academic year, the program officially commenced
through the ,signing of contracts done by the beneficiaries and sponsors.
