Our story

BUTIL: Bonded University Towards Imbued Learning
is a joint scholarship program in cooperation with participating
local student councils,university-wide and college-based organization.
The said program is composed with 70 sponsors
with 70 beneficiaries composing of high school students coming from the
four partner communities of the program namely Lambakin, Sitio Galilee,
Smokey Mountain, and Sta. Rosa II. Read more...

Activities

Hasik:BUTIL Contract Signing
Contract Signing is held every month of June. Beneficiaries renew their memorandum of agreement which will be effective for the whole academic year. Sponsors renew their commitment as well. Annual school supplies are also given to the beneficiaries during the said event

USTbongrunong Leadership Camp
One of four projects of USTbongrunong Students' Initiative. The beneficiaries of which are the BUTIL scholars. The camp is in a form of a leadership training seminar that hones the leadership skills of the BUTIL scholars.

KISLAP:BUTIL Night Race
A BUTIL fundraising event in a form of night race. Proceeds go directly to the allowance of the beneficiaries and fund for other BUTIL projects. Sponsors are expected to take part in the recruitment of runners.

BUKLOD:BUTIL Family Day
A project which aims to build good relationship and bonding between the sponsors and their beneficiaries. Each sponsor is required to send at least one representative to spend the whole day with their beneficiary. A series of games, presentations and activities will be conducted throughout the event.

Allowance Distribution
Allowances are distributed twice per semester. Each sponsor is expected to send one representative who will be scheduled to go to the partner communities together with the other volunteers/representatives to give the allowances to the BUTIL scholars. Short activities are also done by the beneficiaries every allowance distribution.

BUTIL Honesty Kiosk
Additional fund-raising project of the scholarship program in a form of honesty store. It is located at the UST Tan Yan Kee Student Center Lobby.
